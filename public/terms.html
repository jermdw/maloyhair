How you opt in

You opt in by giving verbal consent in person or by phone when scheduling an appointment with Maloy Hair. At that time, your phone number is entered into our scheduling system by Maloy Hair staff so we can send you appointment-related texts.

What we'll send you

  • A confirmation message when your appointment is booked.
  • A reminder approximately 48 hours before your appointment.
  • A reminder approximately 2 hours before your appointment.

You can reply to certain messages to manage your appointment — for example, replying C to confirm or X to cancel. Message frequency varies depending on how many appointments you book; expect up to 3 messages per appointment.

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to stop receiving texts from us. You'll receive one final confirmation message. You can also ask us by phone or email to remove your number — see contact info below. Opting out of texts does not cancel any scheduled appointment; please contact us directly to reschedule or cancel.
